Fun Facts

Inception

Gushers were first introduced in 1991 by Betty Crocker as a type of fruit snack. The candy was an instant hit and quickly became popular among kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

The original flavors of Gushers were strawberry, tropical punch, and grape. Since then, the brand has expanded to include a variety of different flavors such as orange, green apple, and fruit punch.

Taste

In addition to being a popular snack, Gushers have also been used in various recipes such as Gusher-infused cocktails and Gusher-filled cupcakes. Some people even enjoy freezing Gushers and eating them as a cold, refreshing treat.

Bonus

Gushers were once the subject of controversy due to a rumor that the candy contained pig fat. This rumor was later debunked by the company, which confirmed that Gushers are completely vegetarian and do not contain any animal-derived ingredients.
