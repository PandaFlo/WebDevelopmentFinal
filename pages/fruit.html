Fun Facts

Inception

Welch's Fruit Snacks were first introduced in 1985 by the Welch's company, which is known for its grape juice and other fruit-based products. The fruit snacks were created as a way to offer a healthy, convenient snack option for children.

Ingredients

Welch's Fruit Snacks are made with real fruit juice, including grape, raspberry, and cherry. The fruit snacks do not contain any artificial flavors or colors, making them a healthier alternative to many other types of candy.

Taste

Welch's Fruit Snacks have a sweet, fruity taste that is enjoyed by children and adults alike. The fruit snacks come in a variety of flavors and shapes, including strawberry, grape, and peach rings, making them a fun and versatile snack option.

Bonus

Welch's Fruit Snacks have been endorsed by several major health organizations, including the American Heart Association and the National PTA. The fruit snacks have also been featured in popular TV shows and movies, including "Stranger Things" and "The Office."
